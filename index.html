Our Story

At Sequoia Forest Products, we believe in doing one thing exceptionally well: redwood fencing. Our team combines years of forestry knowledge with forward-thinking sustainability. What began as a regional sawmill has grown into a trusted name in redwood fencing, known for product integrity, environmental responsibility, and a deep commitment to the people who build with our boards every day.

The Power of Focus 

Why Sequoia Only Does Fencing

By concentrating exclusively on fencing, we're able to provide quality lumber and results that general lumber suppliers simply can’t match:

  • Higher Consistency: Precision milling ensures uniform boards in every batch.
  • Better Inventory Control: Streamlined operations mean we stock what customers need.
  • Fewer Defects: Quality starts with selective harvesting and continues through every step.
  • Unmatched Expertise: We know fencing inside and out and we share that knowledge with our partners.

The Sequoia Difference

Sequoia is the Fence Industry's redwood specialist with a purpose-built mill, a sustainable mindset, and a product that stands the test of time.

Sequoia Values

What We Stand For

At Sequoia, sustainability isn't a tagline; it's built into our process. Our redwood is harvested through responsible thinning practices that preserve a healthy forestscape, reduce fire risk by removing ladder fuels, and ensure redwood is a sustainable option for years to come. From start to finish, we're committed to zero-waste, zero-emission milling.
